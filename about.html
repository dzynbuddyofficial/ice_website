Our Story

From Cuttack to Bhubaneswar, serving happiness one bite at a time.

How It All Began

Indian Chicken Express (ICE) was born in Cuttack with one mission — to serve the crispiest, juiciest chicken and the cheesiest pizzas at affordable prices. What started as a small kitchen quickly became a favorite for families, friends, and foodies across Odisha.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to make ICE a household name across India, while staying true to our roots in Odisha. We believe in fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and creating moments of joy with every bite. Our mission is simple — to keep serving happiness on every plate.
